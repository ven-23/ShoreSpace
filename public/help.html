Mapping Slots
  • Click Add Slot in the sidebar.
  • Click 4 points on the camera feed to outline a parking space.
  • Right-click any slot to delete it.
  • Drag existing slots to adjust their position.
  • Important: Map empty slots first for better detection accuracy.
Calibration
  • Use Calibrate Cam when lighting conditions change significantly.
  • Ensure the parking lot is empty (or the mapped slots are empty) before calibrating.
  • The system compares the current view against this calibration image to detect cars.
Settings & Pricing
  • Go to Settings to adjust billing rates.
  • Base Rate: The initial fee charged upon exit.
  • Grace Period: Time allowed before hourly charges apply.
  • Hourly Rate: Added for every hour exceeded after the grace period.
Data Management
  • Click Export Layout to save your drawn map to a JSON file.
  • Use Import Layout to restore a previously saved map.
  • Analytics: View revenue charts and occupancy trends in the Analytics dashboard.
  • All transaction history is saved locally in your browser.
